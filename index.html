About me

I want to focus specifically on designing gameplay mechanics for gaming projects. My interest in game design grew as I started working on related projects. Most of my projects are created with Unity or Unreal Engine 5, but I'm eager to learn other engines as well. I'm always excited to work on new projects that teach me new mechanics, languages, and engines. My goal as a game developer is to create a solo game featuring a unique mechanic inspired by most of my friends. This way, when my friends play it, they will recognize all the different references.
