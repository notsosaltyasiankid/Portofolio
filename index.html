About me

I want to focus specifically on developing gameplay mechanics for gaming projects. My interest in game design grew as I started working on related projects. Most of my projects are created with Unity or Unreal Engine 5, but I'm eager to learn other engines as well. I'm always excited to work on new projects that teach me new mechanics, languages, and engines. My goal as a game developer is to create a solo game featuring a unique mechanic inspired by most of my friends. This way, when my friends play it, they will recognize all the different references.

Personal

My full name is Alex Willem Yudistira Satriaji Bons (quite a long name, huh) , and I'm 18 years old. I've lived in the Netherlands my whole life, currently in the village of Hillegom. Hillegom is near Haarlem and is known for its beautiful tulip fields, which are great for photography. I started learning to code from my brother, who inspired me when I saw him build a website from scratch with HTML. Later, I took a class where I could choose between web development or game development. After some thought, I chose game development because I was fascinated by how games create unique mechanics. Most of my time is spent on my PC, doing things I find entertaining—gaming, coding, chatting with friends, watching movies or series, and learning new things. I also enjoy sports, especially basketball, which I have played for almost ten years.
